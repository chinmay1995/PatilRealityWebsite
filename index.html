Our Story

Founded with a singular vision to redefine the real estate landscape, The Patil Realty has been a cornerstone of trust, transparency, and architectural brilliance in Maharashtra. Under the visionary leadership of our founder, Sachin Patil, we have dedicated ourselves to creating not just physical structures, but vibrant communities where families thrive and legacies are born.

From our very first project to our current pan-Maharashtra presence, our journey is defined by an uncompromising commitment to quality materials, strategic locations, and putting the homebuyer's needs first.

Our Vision

To be Maharashtra’s most trusted real estate developer, transforming skylines while building sustainable, world-class living spaces that endure for generations.

Our Mission

To deliver excellence every day through transparent dealings, strategic prime locations, and cutting-edge, RERA-certified construction practices.
