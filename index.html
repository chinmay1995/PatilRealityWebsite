Since 1999

From a Vision to 5,000+ Homes

What began as one man's dream to build dignified, honest homes for Maharashtra's growing middle class has grown into one of the state's most respected real estate groups.

The Patil Realty was built on a single promise: no compromise on quality, no hidden costs, and no broken timelines. Twenty-five years later, that promise remains our North Star.

Today, we operate across multiple cities in Maharashtra — from compact 1 BHK apartments in Nashik to sprawling luxury villas in Kolhapur, and now a Grade-A commercial tower in Mumbai.

1999
Founded in Pune by Sachin Patil. First project: 48 apartments in Kothrud.
2005
Delivered first 500 homes. Expanded to Nashik and Aurangabad.
2012
Awarded 'Best Emerging Developer' by Times Realty. Crossed ₹500 Cr in deliveries.
2018
Launched luxury segment with Serenity Villas, Kolhapur. ISO 9001 certified.
2024
5,000+ families. 8 cities. ₹1,500 Cr+ delivered. Patil Skyline, Mumbai underway.
